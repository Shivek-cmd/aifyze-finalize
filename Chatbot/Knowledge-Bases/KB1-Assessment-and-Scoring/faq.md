# KB1 — FAQs: Assessment & Scoring

> Add each block below as a **separate FAQ entry** in GHL under the "Aifyze — Assessment & Scoring" Knowledge Base.

---

**FAQ: What is the AI Readiness & Opportunity Score?**

It's a personalised score from 0 to 100 that measures how ready your business is to adopt AI with Aifyze's help — and how much opportunity exists. A higher score means clearer pain points, better existing systems, and stronger intent to act, which means faster results. We calculate it from 5 quick questions about your workflows, tools, and goals.

---

**FAQ: How long does the assessment take?**

About 1 to 2 minutes. There are only 5 simple business questions — no technical knowledge required. You get a score snapshot in the chat immediately, and a full personalised report is sent to your email.

---

**FAQ: What do the score bands mean?**

- 0–29 (Very Early): Your business needs more structure and clarity before AI implementation would be effective.
- 30–49 (Emerging): There's genuine interest but some organisation is needed first. A roadmap conversation would help.
- 50–69 (Ready to Pilot): You're in a good position to start with one or two practical AI use cases right away.
- 70–84 (Strong Readiness): Clear opportunities with measurable ROI potential. Implementation can start quickly.
- 85–100 (High-Leverage Ready): Ready for deeper AI implementation and strategic oversight alongside execution.

---

**FAQ: Why does less AI use give a higher score?**

Because the score measures opportunity for Aifyze-guided implementation, not AI maturity. If you're already using AI extensively, there's less room for Aifyze to create new impact. Businesses with little or no current AI use have the most to gain from guided adoption — which is exactly what Aifyze is built for.

---

**FAQ: What happens after I complete the assessment?**

You receive your score snapshot in the chat immediately, then a full personalised report by email. The report covers your score breakdown, top strengths, key gaps, likely AI opportunities, and the Aifyze service path that best fits your situation. The Aifyze team may also follow up if your score indicates strong readiness.
