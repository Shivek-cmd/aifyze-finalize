## Who Is Ritesh Watts?

Ritesh Watts is an Indian-Canadian entrepreneur, author, licensed immigration professional, and speaker. He is the President of Watts Group Ltd. Canada, where he directs strategic vision across all verticals.

His work is built around one core belief: **"Growth should be strategic, not random. AI should create leverage, not noise."**

---

## Background & Credentials

- President, Watts Group Ltd. Canada
- Commissionaire of Oaths, Alberta
- Member, College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC)
- Author of *The DOER Mindset*
- Founder of Watts Technologies (2008, during B.Tech in Computer Science)
- Former IAS officer turned entrepreneur and content creator

His entrepreneurial path began during university, running tech workshops and education promotion initiatives across India. This evolved into a multi-vertical business ecosystem spanning AI, immigration, real estate, community, and education across Canada and India.

## Three Core Pillars of Work

### 1. Build
Business systems, founder mindset, AI leverage, and strategic positioning. Works with founders and business leaders to create scalable, AI-powered operations.

### 2. Move
Career strategy, migration planning, and cross-border opportunities between India and Canada. Licensed immigration consultant with deep expertise in Canada pathways.

### 3. Grow
Wealth-building through business ownership, real estate, and personal development. Advocates for diversified income streams and long-term financial clarity.
