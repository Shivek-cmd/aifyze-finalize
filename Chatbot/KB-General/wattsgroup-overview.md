## What Is Watts Group?

Watts Group Ltd. is a Canadian AI-first consulting and investment group that combines services, technology, capital, and community to empower people and organisations across global markets. It was formally incorporated on April 7, 2021, with entrepreneurial roots dating back to 2008.

**Tagline:** "Power That Empowers"

**Headquarters:** Suite 209, 120 Traders Blvd E, Mississauga, Ontario, L4Z 2H7, Canada

**Service Area:** Canada and internationally

---

## Vision & Mission

**Vision:** Build an AI-first, purpose-led global group that empowers people, businesses, and future-ready ventures with trusted services, strategic guidance, and meaningful opportunities.

**Mission:** Create long-term value through consulting, innovation, operational excellence, and ecosystem support — helping every stakeholder advance with clarity, confidence, and the right resources.

---

## Core Values

- **Purpose with Responsibility** — Business should generate positive outcomes for communities
- **Innovation with Relevance** — Embrace AI and forward-thinking systems where they solve real problems
- **Trust through Action** — Build credibility through consistency and ethical conduct
- **Growth with Clarity** — Pursue intentional, strategic, sustainable development
- **Legacy with Vision** — Honour founding inspiration while building courageously for the future
- **Family-Led, Values-Driven** — Translate legacy and spiritual guidance into responsible business practices

---

## How the Group Creates Value

The group operates through three primary approaches:

1. **Advise & Build** — Consulting and systems across immigration, real estate, technology, education, and media
2. **Operate & Innovate** — Running AI-first, tech-enabled operations solving real-world problems
3. **Back Emerging Ventures** — Supporting founders with strategy, ecosystem access, and long-term guidance
