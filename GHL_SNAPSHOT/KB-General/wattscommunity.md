## What Is Watts Community?

Watts Community is the CSR and professional networking vertical of Watts Group Ltd. Its mission is to create a shared professional environment where growth is accessible to all — not restricted by geography, background, or resources.

**Tagline:** "Build Your Career Network"

**Website:** wattscommunity.in

---

## Four Core Pillars

| Pillar | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Connect** | Curated professional networking across industries |
| **Learn** | Continuous learning through workshops, resources, and events |
| **Grow** | Career advancement via mentorship and business support |
| **Give Back** | Community empowerment as a purpose-led CSR initiative |

---

## Programs & Offerings

- **Member Discussion Forums** — Industry-specific conversations and peer networking
- **Resource Library** — Guides, templates, and playbooks for career and business growth
- **Live Events & Webinars** — Online and in-person workshops with industry experts
- **Mentor Matching** — Connections with vetted professionals for personalised guidance
- **Career Opportunities Board** — Job postings and partnership listings
- **Exclusive Member Resources** — Access to content and tools available only to members

---

## Membership

**One-time membership fee:** CA$100 *(regularly CA$500)*

Includes lifetime access to all features, resources, events, and community forums.

---

## Who It Is For

Professionals, entrepreneurs, students, and newcomers seeking career growth and professional networking — particularly those building careers or businesses across Canada and internationally.
