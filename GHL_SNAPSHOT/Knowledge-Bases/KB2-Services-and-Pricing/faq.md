A fractional AI CEO — sometimes called a Chief AI Officer — is a senior AI leader who works with your business on a part-time or project basis instead of being a full-time hire. They handle AI strategy, vendor selection, team coordination, implementation oversight, and ongoing optimisation. It delivers executive-grade AI leadership at 60–70% less than a full-time hire.
