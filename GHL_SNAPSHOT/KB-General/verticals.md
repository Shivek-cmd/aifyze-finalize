## Overview

Watts Group operates 9 active verticals spanning AI, technology, immigration, real estate, education, entertainment, and community. Each vertical is a focused business unit serving a specific market — all operating under the shared Watts Group umbrella.

---

## The 9 Verticals

---

### 1. Watts Technologies
**Founded:** 2008
**Focus:** Technology and AI solutions

The founding vertical of the group, started by Ritesh Watts during his B.Tech studies. Watts Technologies provides technology consulting, software, and AI-driven systems to businesses and organisations. It is the technical backbone of the group.

---

### 2. Watts Realty
**Founded:** 2014
**Focus:** Real estate and investments

Watts Realty helps clients navigate property investments across Canada and internationally, including the growing Canada–Dubai corridor. Services include investment strategy, property consulting, and portfolio building.

---

### 3. Watts Immigration
**Founded:** 2020
**Focus:** Immigration consulting

Licensed immigration consulting (CICC-registered) for individuals and families seeking Canadian pathways — study permits, work permits, permanent residency, and citizenship. Led by Ritesh Watts, Commissionaire of Oaths, Alberta.

---

### 4. Watts Community
**Founded:** 2023 (expanded)
**Focus:** CSR and professional networking

A purpose-led professional community offering networking, mentorship, career resources, and live events. One-time membership of CA$100 for lifetime access. Website: wattscommunity.in

---

### 5. Abroadiz
**Founded:** 2024
**Focus:** Study abroad consultancy

Helps students plan and apply for international education — particularly Canadian universities. Services include university selection, application guidance, and pre-departure support.

---

### 6. Applyio
**Founded:** 2024
**Focus:** Application management platform

A digital platform for managing study and immigration applications, streamlining the process for students and applicants through organised, trackable workflows.

---

### 7. Aifyze
**Founded:** 2026
**Focus:** AI consulting and automation

Aifyze is the group's dedicated AI consulting vertical. It helps businesses integrate AI into their existing processes without disruption — through workflow automation, AI agents, strategy consulting, and fractional AI leadership. Website: aifyze.com

*For detailed information about Aifyze's services, refer to the Aifyze knowledge base or visit aifyze.com.*

---

### 8. Fun-A-Boo
**Founded:** 2026
**Focus:** Kids entertainment and learning

Led by Mrs. Akanksha Watts, Fun-A-Boo delivers engaging educational entertainment for children. Combines learning with fun through creative content and activities.
