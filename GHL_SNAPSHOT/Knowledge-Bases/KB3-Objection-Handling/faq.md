# KB3 — FAQs: Objection Handling

> Add each block below as a **separate FAQ entry** in GHL under the "Aifyze — Objection Handling" Knowledge Base.
> These are designed to be triggered when the user expresses hesitation, doubt, or pushback.

---

**FAQ: We are too small for AI.**

Not at all. Many small businesses benefit the most from AI because the gains on a small team are proportionally larger. You do not need a large tech budget or a dedicated IT team. Aifyze helps small businesses start with one or two simple use cases — like automating lead follow-up or reducing admin time — that create measurable results from week one. The goal is leverage, not complexity.

---

**FAQ: We are not technical enough to use AI.**

That is completely fine — and it is actually why Aifyze exists. You do not need to understand how AI works to benefit from it. Aifyze handles the technical side entirely. Your job is to tell us the problems; our job is to solve them with AI in a way your team can use without any technical knowledge.

---

**FAQ: We do not want to replace our current systems or tools.**

You do not need to. That is exactly Aifyze's approach. We integrate AI into the tools and processes you already use — not alongside a full system overhaul. If you use a CRM, we connect AI to it. If you manage things in spreadsheets, we automate around that. Nothing gets replaced unless you want it to.

---

**FAQ: We are just exploring and not ready to commit.**

That is the perfect time to do a readiness check. This assessment gives you a clear, no-obligation baseline so you know where you stand, what your biggest opportunity is, and what a sensible first step would look like — without any pressure to move forward. Many of our best clients started as explorers.

---

**FAQ: Why do you need my email address?**

We send your full AI Readiness & Opportunity Score report and personalised recommendations by email so you have everything in one place. It means you can review the full report later and share it with your team. We do not spam — the only follow-up from Aifyze is relevant to your results.

---

**FAQ: Can you just tell me my score here without the email?**

I can share a brief snapshot in the chat right now. However, the full personalised report — which includes your score breakdown, top strengths, key gaps, best AI opportunities, and the right service path for your situation — is only delivered by email so it stays organised and shareable. It takes seconds to enter your email and the report arrives within minutes.

---

**FAQ: We have no budget for AI right now.**

That is worth knowing. Aifyze's process automation work starts from $500 and is scoped to your exact situation — you only pay for what you actually need. Many clients find that the time savings from even one automated workflow more than cover the cost within the first month. A free AI audit helps you see the numbers before committing to anything.

---

**FAQ: We tried AI tools before and they did not work.**

That is more common than people realise — and usually it comes down to choosing the wrong tool for the job, or implementing without a clear workflow strategy. Aifyze's approach starts with understanding your specific processes first, then selects and configures tools around those processes. The result is AI that actually fits how your business works, not generic software that requires your team to adapt to it.

---

**FAQ: How do we know AI will actually save us time?**

Every Aifyze engagement is tied to measurable outcomes before work begins. In discovery, we identify the specific workflows being automated, estimate time savings, and project ROI. You know what you are getting before you invest. Clients typically see results within 30 days of a pilot.

---

**FAQ: We are worried about AI making mistakes or giving wrong information to our customers.**

That is a valid concern and one Aifyze takes seriously. AI agents are configured with guardrails — they only respond within the scope of what they are trained on, and escalate to a human when a question falls outside their confidence threshold. You always have oversight and control. No AI is deployed without testing and your sign-off.

---

**FAQ: We do not have time to implement something new right now.**

Aifyze's implementation is designed to be low-friction. The average pilot takes 2–3 weeks to set up, requires minimal involvement from your team, and runs alongside your current operations. We do the heavy lifting. The time you invest upfront is small compared to the time saved ongoing.

---

**FAQ: We already have someone handling this internally.**

That is great — Aifyze can work alongside your internal team rather than replacing them. Many clients use Aifyze as an extension of their team for specialised AI implementation that would take internal staff much longer to figure out independently. We accelerate what your team is already trying to do.
